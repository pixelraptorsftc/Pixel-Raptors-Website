What We Do

Pixel Raptors combines mechanical design, software, and teamwork to compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge. We prototype quickly, iterate often, and treat every match as data.

Engineering

Robot Design & Build

From mecanum drive to complex scoring mechanisms, our robots are designed for agility, reliability, and continuous improvement.

Software

Autonomous & Control

We write our own control software, exploring vision, advanced pathfinding, and robust tele-op code for consistent performance.

Community

Outreach & Impact

Beyond the field, we share robotics with our community through demos, outreach, and mentoring future teams.
