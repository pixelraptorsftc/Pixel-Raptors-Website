Awards & Outreach Events

Open event at Bothell Library- November 30th, 2025

We reached around 100 people during this outreach opportunity, and our group worked hard to entertain the guests that came to listen about the Pixel Raptors Team. Many of the guests with younger kids also asked about the FLL (First Lego Leauge), and we provided them with extra information about that. Overall, it was a educating and fun day, and we enjoyed teaching the people who came about FTC robotics.
